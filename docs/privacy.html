Privacy Policy — BT MFG Invoicer

Last updated: June 10, 2026

BT MFG Invoicer ("the App") is a private, internal tool operated by BT Manufacturing ("we", "us") to create invoices in our own QuickBooks Online company from our internal job records. The App is not offered to, installed by, or used by any third party.

Information we access

When connected to QuickBooks Online via Intuit's authorized OAuth 2.0 flow, the App reads and writes accounting data in BT Manufacturing's own company file only — specifically customers, products/services (items), and invoices — for the sole purpose of generating sales invoices.

How we use it

Accessed data is used only to look up the correct customer and item and to create or update invoices that match our purchase orders. We do not use QuickBooks data for advertising, profiling, or any unrelated purpose.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share QuickBooks data with any third party. Data remains between BT Manufacturing's systems and Intuit's QuickBooks Online service. No data is transmitted to any other external service.

Data storage and security

The App runs locally on BT Manufacturing's own computer. OAuth tokens are stored in a local configuration file on that machine and are not shared. We retain only the minimum data needed to perform invoicing and delete local working files when no longer needed.

Token revocation

Access can be revoked at any time from within QuickBooks Online (Apps → Connected apps) or by disconnecting the App, which invalidates its tokens.
